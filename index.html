Introduction to Computer Science

Introduction to Computer Science is the prerequisite for Computer and Information Science
in grade 12. In this course we learn the basics of languages that are used to create websites,
such as : HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Later in the course we will learn to program using a
programming language called Python.

Chemistry

Grade 11 Chemistry is the prerequisite for grade 12 Chemistry. In this course we depen our
understanding of chemistry through the study of the properties of chemicals, chemical bonds,
and chemical reactions. We will further develop our analytical skill and investigate the
qualitative and quantitative properties of matter.

AP Functions

Grade 11 AP Functions is the prerequisite for grade 12 AP Advanced Functions. In this
course we will be introduce to mathematical concept of the function by extending our
experiences with linear and quadratic relations. We will investigate discrete and continuous
functions such as trigonometry and exponential functions.

French

Grade 11 French is the prerequisite for grade 12 French. In this course we will have
opportunities to consolidate the communication skills required to speak and interact with
increasing confidence and accuracy in French in a variety of academic and social contexts.
We will use our skills of reading, writing, listening, and speaking and apply language learning
strategies while exploring a variety of concrete and abstract topics
